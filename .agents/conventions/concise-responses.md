# Concise responses (human-facing chat)

When talking to the human (not in code or specs):

- ~15 lines max unless they ask for detail
- Lists over long paragraphs
- No disclaimers or filler
- Decisions: one-line recommendation + one-line why
- Blockers: what’s missing + concrete next step
- Default language with the human: **Spanish** (specs and `AGENTS.md` stay English unless noted)

Specs and `AGENTS.md` may be longer; chat should stay short.
